About me

I am what they call "an extreme minimalist".

I value minimalism in every aspect of my life, but only manage to apply it in my work (if I can convince other people).

I like to be constrained by a set of boundaries while trying to find a solution to a problem. I like when things are simple and succinct. I like to have a solid mental model of the systems I'm working on. I like to have full control. In other words, I have OCD.

My mission

I am interested in: DAOs, Zero-knowledge Proofs, AGI, Soft Robots, Neural Interfaces. I am continuously thinking about how to apply these technologies to build a better future.

My current plan is:

  1. Build a DAO framework - solve the coordination of distributed agents.
  2. DAO for AGI - the most powerful technology has to be in the hands of the community.
  3. DAO for Soft Robots - with the help of AI, build a general-purpose humanoid robot.
  4. DAO for Neural Interfaces - connect/upload the mind with/into the computer.

Happy to discuss any of these topics.
